FAQ

What age should my child start piano?

The typical starting age is anywhere from 5-8 years old, but it really depends.

If your child can sit down for a period of time and is open to having some fun then let's get started!

What are your credentials? How long have you been teaching?

I've been teaching for over 30 years now, and am a RMT (registered music teacher) and a part of the CFMTA (Canadian Federation of Music Teacher's Association).
I have a Bachelor's Degree in Music and have completed my ARCT for teaching.

See the About Me section of my website for more information.

What levels do you teach?

I teach students of all levels!
Whether you're a beginner or working towards your level 10 certificate, this is a place for you.

I teach for both the RCM (Royal Conservatory of Music) and the CC (Canadian Conservatory). It is completely, 100% the student's choice whether they would like to partake in a Conservatory curriculum, or simply embrace the joy of music.

What are you billing cycles/cancellation policies?

Payment is made by post-dated cheques.

At least one month's advance notice must be given before termination of all lessons, after which the cheques dated for the next month would be returned.

My policies align similarly to that recommended by the BCRMTA/ORMTA. Feel free to send an inquiry above for more information!

Where and what do you teach?

Currently I teach at 3530 Sun Hills, Victoria. Click the location link above for the Google Maps! Recitals will take place in an alternate location with a larger venue, including churches etc.

I teach beginner to advanced piano, along with basic to advanced rudiments in music theory.

What's your approach to teaching?

All lessons are tailored to the student as everyone learns differently. Which I believe is a fundamental aspect to good teaching.

I take a supportive and fun approach to teaching while delivering the knowledge and feedback easily and efficiently, in a way the student can really understand.
