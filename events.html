EXAMS

Jane helps to prepare students in fulfilling examination
requirements for both the Royal Conservatory of music (RCM)
as well as the Canadian Conservatory.

Exams are not mandatory! Nevertheless, they do provide certification,
that can be transferable into high school credits as well as
application and resume documents.Exams also provide preparation
for the pursuit of post-secondary music degrees and professions.

Exams are available in a variety of levels from the basic
1 to 10 for both conservatories, to pedagogy
and associate diplomas (ARCT).

Check out the About page for exam stories!

RECITALS

Jane has bi-annual recitals in June and December.
Each and every student is strongly encouraged to perform in the
recitals, as they provide excellent stage experience.

Recitals foster performance abilities, helping to overcome
stage fright and learn memory and adaptability techniques.
Recitals cultivate musicianship and give students a chance to get
real world experience, prepare for examinations and showcase their abilities.

Jane can also prepare her students for other recitals that take
place throughout the city.
